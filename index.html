Hey! I'm Mehul, currently a senior at UC Berkeley studying Materials Science and Nuclear Engineering. Throughout my four years at Berkeley, I've been part of a few research labs and have done research in Electron Microscopy, Nuclear Materials, Ferroelectrics, Materials for Extreme Environments, Applied Superconductivity and Radiation Detection. I've loved both theoretical and experimental aspects of my research and my aim is to bridge the two through computational methods for electron microscopy. Beyond the lab I love going to art museums, 3D modeling and I've been getting into crocheting!

As for this site (or as long as it stays in this iteration), I've mostly designed it so that I can share a bit more about myself and what I think is cool. Feel free to click around and if you have any specific questions feel free to contact me!
